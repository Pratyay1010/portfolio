SONY SSUP RESEARCH INTERN • IIT DELHI

Embedded Vision for Autonomous Additive Manufacturing

  • Worked on real-time defect detection for autonomous multi-axis additive manufacturing using Sony IMX500 AI camera hardware and embedded computer vision pipelines.
  • Explored lightweight detection and segmentation architectures for deployment-aware perception under strict edge-compute, latency, and memory constraints.
  • Investigated quantized inference, ROI-based preprocessing, and multi-camera monitoring pipelines for robust in-situ defect detection during active printing operations.
  • Evaluated the trade-off between perception accuracy, inference stability, and hardware efficiency for reliable edge AI deployment in autonomous manufacturing environments.
